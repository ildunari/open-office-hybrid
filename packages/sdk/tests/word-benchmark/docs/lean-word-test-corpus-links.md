# Lean Word Test-Corpus Links

This is the **lean first-pass corpus** for the Word-agent benchmark zip. It favors broad failure-mode coverage over collecting every possible template on earth.

## Why this pack is lean but useful

- It gives you one **clean long-document academic** seed.
- One **scientific manuscript** seed.
- One **grant/compliance-heavy** seed plus one grant-adjacent biosketch artifact.
- One **report/office-document** seed.
- Two distinct **legal** tracks: contract-style and pleading-style.
- One **review-state** seed for comments and tracked changes.

That is enough coverage to expose most first-order Word-agent failure modes without turning the starter corpus into a giant template landfill.
